Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Student Portal

Registration & Login

To register for the Student Portal:

  1. Go to the Registration page
  2. Fill in all required fields marked with *
  3. Use your official student ID (e.g., 24cs105)
  4. Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
  5. Agree to terms and conditions
  6. Click "Register Now"

You'll receive a confirmation email once registration is complete.

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the Login page
  2. Click on "Forgot your password?"
  3. Enter your email address or student ID
  4. Check your email for reset instructions
  5. Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Technical Issues

If the portal is running slowly, try these steps:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Close unnecessary browser tabs
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try using a different browser
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily
  • Restart your browser

If the issue persists, contact technical support.

The Student Portal supports the following browsers:

  • Google Chrome (recommended) - version 90+
  • Mozilla Firefox - version 88+
  • Microsoft Edge - version 90+
  • Safari - version 14+

For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of Google Chrome.

When encountering error messages:

  1. Note down the exact error message
  2. Take a screenshot if possible
  3. Try refreshing the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
  4. Clear your browser cache
  5. Try logging out and logging back in
  6. If the error persists, contact support with the error details

Academic Information

To view your academic records:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to your Profile page
  3. Click on the "Academic" tab
  4. View your current courses, grades, and GPA

You can also access detailed grade reports from the Dashboard.

GPA (Grade Point Average) is calculated using a 10-point scale:

  • A+ = 10 points
  • A = 9 points
  • A- = 8 points
  • B+ = 7 points
  • B = 6 points
  • B- = 5 points
  • C = 4 points

Your GPA is the weighted average of all your course grades based on credit hours.

Events & Activities

To register for events:

  1. Visit the Events page
  2. Browse available events or use filters
  3. Click on an event to view details
  4. Click the "Register" button
  5. Confirm your registration

You'll receive a confirmation email with event details.

Yes, you can cancel your event registration up to 24 hours before the event start time. Go to your Dashboard, find the event under "Registered Events," and click "Cancel Registration." Note that some events may have different cancellation policies.

Event updates are sent via:

  • Email notifications (if enabled in settings)
  • Dashboard announcements
  • SMS alerts for urgent updates (if phone number provided)

You can customize notification preferences in your Profile settings.
